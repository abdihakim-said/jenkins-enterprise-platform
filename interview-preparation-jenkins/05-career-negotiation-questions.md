### **Preparation Checklist:**
- [ ] Research company, team, and interviewers (LinkedIn, company blog)
- [ ] Prepare 3-5 detailed project stories using STAR method
- [ ] Practice technical explanations with diagrams
- [ ] Prepare thoughtful questions for each interviewer
- [ ] Review your Jenkins project architecture and metrics

### **Day-of Strategy:**
1. **Arrive Early**: 10-15 minutes for mental preparation
2. **Bring Materials**: Portfolio, architecture diagrams, project summaries
3. **Energy Management**: Stay hydrated, take breaks between interviews
4. **Consistency**: Tell the same project stories consistently
5. **Engagement**: Show genuine interest and enthusiasm
6. **Follow-up**: Send thank you notes within 24 hours

### **Post-Interview Actions:**
1. **Immediate Notes**: Write down key points and impressions
2. **Thank You Messages**: Personalized to each interviewer
3. **Follow-up Questions**: Send any additional information requested
4. **Decision Timeline**: Clarify next steps and timeline
5. **Continued Interest**: Stay engaged throughout the process
